Support Our Work

Your generous contribution directly funds skills training, medical support, advocacy campaigns, and Christian fellowship for families and caregivers of children living with disabilities.

Your Impact

When you give to Hope Beyond Disability, you are investing in a future where stigma is dismantled and families are built up. 100% of your donation goes directly toward funding local community development operations in Kenya.

What your donation can buy:

  • $25 feeds a caregiver and child during a weekend discipleship seminar.
  • $50 buys craft and vocational materials to train one caregiver in soap-making or beadwork.
  • $150 sponsors a community-wide disability awareness workshop.
  • $500 provides a micro-grant and business startup kit for three caregivers to launch their own shops.
Confirming Your Gift

After making your contribution, please send a screenshot of the M-Pesa confirmation message or details of your bank transfer via WhatsApp to +254 700 000 000 or email info@hopebeyonddisability.org so we can send you an official donation receipt and acknowledgment.
