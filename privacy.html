Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026. Your privacy is paramount. Read below to understand how we collect, store, and process your information.

1. Overview

Hope Beyond Disability is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, partners, volunteers, and beneficiaries. This Privacy Policy details our practices concerning data collection, protection, and usage through our online platform and physical outreach operations.

2. Information Collection

We may collect personal details when you engage with us, including:

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number).
  • Donation details (records of financial support and gift preferences).
  • Communications (records of inquiry emails, form submissions, and feedback).

We do NOT store credit card numbers, bank credentials, or mobile money PINs on our website. All electronic payments are processed securely via verified third-party gateways.

3. Data Utilization

Your information is used solely to:

  • Acknowledge and process your donations and partnerships.
  • Provide operational updates, news, and ministry reports.
  • Respond to feedback, comments, and volunteer applications.
  • Comply with local Kenyan Community Based Organisation regulations and auditing guidelines.

4. Data Safeguarding

We implement top-tier technical and administrative security measures to prevent unauthorized access, alternation, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data. We never sell, trade, or rent personal information to external commercial third parties.

5. Contact Information

If you have questions about this policy or request deletion/rectification of your data, please contact us at info@hopebeyonddisability.org.
