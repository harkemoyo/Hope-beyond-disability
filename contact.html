Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a registered organization?

Yes, Hope Beyond Disability is fully registered in Kenya as a Community Based Organization (CBO). We operate under local social development regulations and maintain strict audit and compliance standards.

Where are you located?

Our administrative office and primary community outreach center are located in Nairobi, Kenya (specifically Karen and surrounding municipal areas). We conduct workshops and host caregiver groups throughout these regions.

Can I donate non-monetary items?

Absolutely! We welcome in-kind donations such as physical mobility aids (wheelchairs, crutches, walking frames), educational toys, sensory learning items, children's clothes, and vocational training supplies (sewing machines, scissors, sewing fabrics). Please email us at info@hopebeyonddisability.org to coordinate drop-off or pickup.

How are financial donations utilized?

100% of public donations are used directly on the ground to fund caregiver vocational skills training, family discipleship retreats, purchasing mobility aids for children, and staging community anti-stigma awareness campaigns.

Building compassionate communities where every child is valued and every caregiver is supported.
