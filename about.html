About Hope Beyond Disability (HBD)

Founded in 2025, Hope Beyond Disability is a registered Community Based Organization that exists to respond to the persistent stigmatization, limited awareness, and inadequate advocacy experienced by children living with disabilities and their caregivers. These challenges often result in social exclusion, poor caregiving support, and limited opportunities for children to thrive.

HBD addresses these problems by promoting disability awareness, equipping caregivers with practical skills and training, and advocating for an inclusive society that upholds the dignity and wellbeing of people with disabilities.

Vision

An inclusive society where children living with disabilities flourish, supported by informed and empowered caregivers.

Mission

To promote awareness, empower caregivers, and advocate for the rights and wellbeing of people with disabilities.

Main Objectives

1

To strengthen the spiritual well-being of caregivers through Christ-centered discipleship.

2

To advance inclusive policies and practices that protect the rights and dignity of children with disabilities and improve access to services and opportunities.

3

To transform community attitudes by reducing stigma and promoting understanding, acceptance, and inclusion of children with disabilities and their families.

4

To equip caregivers with practical skills, resources, and support systems that strengthen caregiving capacity, economic stability, and family resilience.

Core Values

  • 1. Compassion We intentionally act by recognizing the suffering, needs, and vulnerabilities of others and responding with empathy, care, and practical action.
  • 2. Dignity We treat people not as statistics, projects, or recipients of aid, but as valuable human beings deserving honor, respect, and inclusion.
  • 3. Impact We are committed to creating lasting transformation through intentional, effective, and results-driven actions.
  • 4. Integrity We are committed to honesty, transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct in all our actions and decisions.

Building compassionate communities where every child is valued and every caregiver is supported.
