About Hope Beyond Disability

Learn more about our foundation, our commitment, and the values that drive our support for children with disabilities and their caregivers.

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2025, Hope Beyond Disability (HBD) is a registered Community Based Organisation (CBO). Our vision is a society where every child living with a disability is valued, loved, and given the opportunity to reach their full potential, and where caregivers are fully empowered and supported.

We work relentlessly to break down social stigma, eliminate exclusion, and offer holistic community-based programs that address the physical, spiritual, social, and economic needs of caregivers and their children.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion Guided by faith, we serve families with empathy, kindness, and deep love, meeting them in their moments of greatest need.
  • Dignity & Respect We believe every child is made in the image of God and is worthy of absolute respect, rights, inclusion, and honor.
  • Empowerment We equip caregivers with skills, livelihood training, and support systems to move them from isolation to self-reliance.
  • Community & Unity We build networks of solidarity to stand with caregivers, combating isolation through collective advocacy and Christ-centered fellowship.

Why We Exist

Across many communities, children living with developmental, cognitive, or physical disabilities face immense social barriers. Deep-seated cultural stigma often leaves families isolated. Mothers, who are usually the primary caregivers, frequently face abandonment, economic strain, and emotional exhaustion.

HBD exists to stand in the gap. We believe that with the right support, community training, and civic advocacy, we can shift societal attitudes and create an environment where every child can grow, learn, and be celebrated.

Building compassionate communities where every child is valued and every caregiver is supported.
